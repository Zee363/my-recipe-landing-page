Pasta Is Always A Good Idea!

Beef Lasagna Recipe

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 200°C. Finely chop the carrots and garlic in a food processor, then soften in the olive oil over a low heat for 5 minutes carefully monitoring that they do not change colour. Increase the heat to medium then add the mince. Brown the mince then add thyme and red wine and cook for 1-2 minutes.
Stir through the tomato puree, canned tomatoes, beef stock, tomato paste and season to taste. Let it simmer for 45 mimutes to an hour, then stir through the balsamic vinegar.

To make the white sauce, melt the butter in a pan over low heat, then whisk in the flour and cook for a minute. Slowly add the milk, a little at a time, and whisk into the flour mixture until smooth. Simmer for a further minute stirring continously and add season to taste.

Place a layer of mince in the of an overproof dish, then top with a layer of pasta and white sauce. Repeat until the dish is full, finishing with a layer of white sauce. Sprinkle over the Parmigiano Reggiano, paprika and breadcrumbs. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden.

Enjoy! 🤤

Ingredients:

  1. Two large onions (peeled)
  2. Two larged carrots (peeled)
  3. Four garlic cloves (peeled & crushed)
  4. Two tablespoons of olive oil
  5. Free-range lean beef mince (1kg)
  6. Two sprigs of fresh thyme
  7. 1/4Cup of red wine (optional)
  8. 1x Can tomato puree (410g)
  9. 1x Can chopped tomatoes (410g)
  10. 1/2Cup beef-stock (good-quality)
  11. One tablespoon tomato paste
  12. Sea salt & freshly ground black pepper for taste
  13. One tablespoon of balsamic vinegar
  14. Dried or fresh lasagna sheets, cooked and drained (300-400g)
  15. Parmigiano Reggiano, grated paprika for sprinkling (100g)
  16. Breadcrumbs (100g)

For the white sauce:

  1. Butter (75g)
  2. Flour (75g)
  3. Two cups of milk
  4. Sea salt & freshly ground black pepper to taste
